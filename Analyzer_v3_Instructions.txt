# Skill Transition Burden (STB)

STB = **How difficult it will be for today’s workforce to adapt if the task is significantly transformed by AI**.  
This score is **independent** of the AI Impact Risk score. It reflects the *adaptability burden*, not task complexity or automation likelihood.

### Step 1: Assess the Nature of Role Transformation

Base score on the expected changes to the human role, as described in the long-form narrative:

| AI Transformation Scenario                                                             | STB Baseline |
|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------|
| Minimal augmentation; humans perform task mostly the same                              | 1–2          |
| Humans supervise AI output, flag exceptions, apply judgment                            | 3–5          |
| Task shifts to require new tools, interfaces, or analytical interpretation             | 6–8          |
| AI creates a fundamentally new task for humans (e.g., trainer, pattern recognizer)     | 8–10         |

Do not consider how likely this is to occur — only how hard it would be to adapt **if** it occurs.

### Step 2: Adjust for Fit Between New Role and Current Worker Profile

| Indicator                                                           | STB Modifier |
|---------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------|
| AI-enhanced role is similar to current skills/tools/processes       | –2 to –1     |
| New role requires digital fluency, data judgment, or interface logic| +1 to +3     |
| AI shifts role from judgment/communication to tagging/review tasks  | +2           |
| Human oversight of AI becomes central to task                       | +1 to +2     |

### Step 3: Apply Modifiers from Mapper Experience Zone and Tenure

| Attribute      | STB Modifier |
|----------------|--------------|
| EZ 1–2         | +1           |
| EZ 3–4         | 0            |
| EZ 5           | –1 (if aligned), or +1 (if misaligned) |

| Tenure Band    | STB Modifier |
|----------------|--------------|
| <3 years       | –1           |
| 4–9 years      | 0            |
| 10+ years      | +1           |

### Output Format:
Final STB must be an integer from **1 to 10**, where:
- 1–3 = Low Burden → high adaptability
- 4–6 = Moderate Burden → moderate retraining
- 7–10 = High Burden → deep reskilling, difficult transformation

STB feeds the **Y-axis of the Quadrant Chart** and must be evaluated independently of Risk.
